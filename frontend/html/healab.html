Our Products

HEALOFINAX-SP

Healofinac-SP combines Aceclofenac, Paracetamol, and Serratiopeptidase to repair tissues and reduce inflammation. Aceclofenac supports cartilage health, Paracetamol provides pain relief, and Serratiopeptidase reduces swelling and improves micro-circulation for faster healing, ideal for conditions like dental pain, rheumatoid arthritis, and postoperative pain.

GASTROLAB-IV INJECTION

Gastrolab-IV (Pantoprazole 40 mg for Injection) provides rapid, effective control of gastric hyperacidity and heartburn in patients with acute or chronic gastritis, GERD, and peptic ulcers. It reduces gastric acid secretion, lowers ulcer bleeding risk, and shortens hospital stays by promoting faster symptom relief. This injection offers targeted, immediate relief for critical acid-related conditions.

MORBEHEAL-DDS SUSPENSION

MORBEHEAL DDS features Amoxycillin + Potassium Clavulanate Oral Suspension (457 mg) and is highly effective against strains of Haemophilus influenzae. It is specifically recommended for treating infections such as ENT, respiratory, urinary tract, skin and soft tissue, dental, and bone and joint infections. The medicine is backed by clinical evidence, establishing it as a credible antibiotic option for pediatric patients.

MONTOHEAL-LC SUSPENSION

MONTOHEAL-LC, a combination of Montelukast Sodium and Levocetirizine Hydrochloride, is a broad-spectrum anti-allergy solution. Levocetirizine relieves nasal congestion, while Montelukast reduces inflammation. It provides bronchodilation within two hours, easing symptoms like coughing and wheezing. It is ideal for managing seasonal or perennial allergic rhinitis, mild to moderate asthma, and nasal congestion.

MORBEHEAL-CV 625

MORBEHEAL-CV 625 Tablet is used for the treatment of various bacterial infections such as infection of lungs, airways, ears, urinary tract, skin, bone, joints, soft tissue and tooth. It is a combination medicine containing amoxicillin and clavulanic acid, which belongs to the class of antibiotics.

FEBRIHEAL-250 SUSPENSION

Febriheal-250 offers quick and effective relief from pain and fever. Its blend of paracetamol, phenylephrine, and chlorpheniramine helps reduce discomfort, controls swelling, and restores a sense of well-being. Specially formulated for children, it safely addresses symptoms and brings rapid comfort. Trusted by caregivers, Febriheal-250 is a reliable solution for managing fever and body aches.
